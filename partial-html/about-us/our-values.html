Values We Offer

Act with integrity

Always make ethical decisions in the best interest of our customers, colleagues, and communities

Trust is our currency

Be able to rely on each other

Think big

Continuously strive to scale up our outcomes and impact by 2x, 10x, and 100x

Idea meritocracy

Put egos aside to maximize how much we learn from each other and ‘let the best ideas win’

Use your voice

Be engaged in discussions and come prepared with strong opinions, loosely held

Driver mindset

Take complete ownership in turning problems into solutions and ideas into outcomes

Move fast and break things

Operate with a sense of urgency. We can work towards perfection, but mistakes are inevitable

Customer obsession

Seek to understand our customers, wow them, and use their words to guide our decisions

Embrace feedback

View feedback as a gift, relentlessly seek it, and use it to grow It’s our duty to make this a priority for each other

Empower each other

Elevate those around you and challenge them to step outside their comfort zone
